About LAXMI INDUSTRIES

LAXMI INDUSTRIES was founded in 1994 by Mr. Shankar Patil, a visionary farmer and engineer. Located in MIDC Satara, we have grown into a trusted name for agricultural machinery across Western Maharashtra. Our 20,000 sq. ft. workshop houses state-of-the-art lathe, milling, grinding, and assembly units.

We specialize in rice mills, flour mills, Delaval milking machines, cream separators, shaft keyway cutters, papad machines, pasteurizers, and custom fabrication. As an authorized Delaval service partner, we provide installation and maintenance for dairy equipment. Our team of 18 skilled technicians ensures every machine meets rigorous quality standards.
